How It Works

1. Wrap Tokens

Convert your ERC-20 tokens into Meta tokens with time-lock functionality.

2. Lock Tokens

Choose the lock period for your tokens, ensuring they're secure for future use.

3. Unwrap Tokens

Unwrap your Meta tokens back into the original tokens once the lock period expires.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MetaClone?

MetaClone is a DeFi protocol allowing you to wrap and lock ERC-20 tokens for a specified period.

Can I change the lock time?

No, once the tokens are locked, the time cannot be changed.

Which tokens are supported?

MetaClone supports all standard ERC-20 tokens such as USDC, DAI, and more.
